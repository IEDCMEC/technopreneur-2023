Who We Are

Technopreneur 2023

Technopreneur is the annual e-summit hosted by the Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Cell (IEDC) of Govt. Model Engineering College that brings together entrepreneurs, students, and professionals to network, collaborate, and learn from one another.

Technopreneur is a celebration of entrepreneurship that features a range of activities, including workshops, speaker sessions, competitions, and a startup expo. It offers a wealth of information, resources, and inspiration, and is an excellent opportunity to connect with like-minded individuals and learn from experienced entrepreneurs, professionals, and groundbreakers.

This year marks the 13th edition of the summit, focusing on “Navigating the Unknown”. The theme of this year's Technopreneur also emphasizes the importance of learning from failures and using them as opportunities to grow and improve. Technopreneur aims to help early-stage founders to stay ahead of the curve and build successful businesses by recognizing, and overcoming their mistakes, and obstacles.
